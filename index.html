Our Story

Born from a Love
of Good Coffee

Urban Brew Café started as a modest corner stall in 2018, fuelled by an obsession with the perfect espresso and a deep belief that a café should be more than just a place to grab a drink — it should be a community anchor.

Every bean we use is sourced directly from small, family-run farms across India, Ethiopia, and Colombia. We visit our suppliers, know their names, and pay fair prices. From soil to cup, we believe in a chain of care that you can taste.

Our space was designed with sustainability at its core: reclaimed wood, energy-efficient lighting, compostable packaging, and a zero-single-use-plastic policy. Because a great cup of coffee shouldn't cost the earth.

Our Values

More Than Just Coffee

Locally Sourced

Every ingredient is traced to its source. We partner directly with 12+ small farms, paying above fair-trade prices and visiting each partner annually.

Eco-Friendly Space

Reclaimed timber furniture, LED lighting, rainwater harvesting, and a fully compostable packaging system. Our carbon footprint shrinks every year.

Freshly Roasted

Our in-house roastery produces small-batch roasts twice weekly. No stale warehouse stock — just beans roasted days ago, at peak flavour.

Community First

Monthly open mics, free workspace for freelancers on Tuesdays, and a 'pay-it-forward' wall where customers can buy a coffee for someone in need.
